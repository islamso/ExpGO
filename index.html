About Exp GO!

Exp GO! is a service intended to provide opportunities for students with a lack of real world work experience, while also making life a little easier for companies looking for some extra short-term manpower.

Students

We provide a platform for you to easily browse through potential job openings from companies willing to give inexperienced students a chance. You can browse, write your application and get feedback from the company all in one place!

Companies

Our service allows you to post your job opening in just a few short steps. You can then seamlessly screen through all of the applicants and get in touch with any potential candiates, all without leaving this page.
